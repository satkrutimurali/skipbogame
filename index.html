Skip-Bo

3-player Skip-Bo with 30-card stock piles, 4 discard piles each, and 4 shared building piles.

Draw up to 5 cards at the start of your turn. Play from your hand, the top of your stock pile, or the top of any personal discard pile onto the shared building piles. Discard one hand card to a personal discard pile to end your turn.
